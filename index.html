Our Investment philosophy

Our investment philosophy is to balance growth and risk which means investing in high quality investments. We want our clients to feel comfortable and reassured regarding their investment decision. We also embrace superior client service and discretion. We provide stability and continuity through our results-oriented team of experienced and dedicated professionals who are committed to the client and the firm for the long run. ​ We are also a regulated entity by the AOOS (Aufsichtsorganisation für Vermögensverwalter und Trustees), whose Association is supervised by the Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA). ​ In addition, we have delegated the risk management and compliance functions to the company BV & Compliance Sagl. in Lugano, Switzerland.
